A blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and religious sites

Danteshwari Mata Temple

This temple is one of the 52 Shakti Peethas and is dedicated to the most renowned goddess of Bastar. Special celebrations are held here during Chaitra and Sharadiya Navratri.

Dholkal Ganesh

Located at an altitude of 3,000 feet above sea level, this ancient idol is a trekking destination for adventure enthusiasts.

Barsur

The ancient capital of Nagvanshi king Banasur, famous for its historic temples and 147 Shivlings.

Phulpad Waterfall

Located amidst lush green forests, this waterfall is an ideal spot for nature lovers.

Samlur Shiva Temple

Located about 9 kilometers from Dantewada, this ancient Shiva temple is a significant site for religious tourists.

Bacheli

This place is famous for NMDC's iron ore mining plant and serves as an example of modern development.

Malangir Waterfall

Situated amidst dense forests and mountains, the water of this waterfall is cold and pure, making it a major attraction for tourists.

Indravati River

This major river of Dantewada is known for its scenic beauty and tranquil surroundings. It is ideal for kayaking and photography.

Jhraralawa Waterfall

This waterfall is located amidst forests and is known for its picturesque surroundings. It is ideal for picnics.

Tongpal Hat Bazar

This market is famous for local handicrafts and traditional goods. Tourists can purchase unique items here.

Handawada Waterfall
